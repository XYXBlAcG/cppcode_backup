### 解答 4.2.5.1

$\sum\limits^{n}_{i=1}i^3$

$=\frac{1}{4}((n+1)^4-\sum\limits^{2}_{j=0}C^j_3\sum\limits^n_{i=1}i^j)$

$=\frac{1}{4}(n^4+6\cdot \frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}-4\cdot\frac{n(n+1)}{2}+n)$

$=\frac{1}{4}n^4+\frac{1}{2}n^3+\frac{1}{4}n^2$

$=\frac{n^2(n+1)^2}{4}$

$=(\frac{n(n+1)}{2})^2$

$\Theta (n^4)$
